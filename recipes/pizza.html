Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough and place it on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the cheese and pepperoni on top.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
